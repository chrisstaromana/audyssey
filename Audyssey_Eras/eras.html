Change Artist

Kendrick Lamar wrote about culture, politics, and identity, vividly depicting modern Black American life and critiquing systemic oppression.

  • 2012: Kendrick released good kid, m.A.A.d city, his breakthrough album to global fame and critical acclaim.
  • 2015: To Pimp a Butterfly pushed boundaries in genre and social commentary, cementing him as a cultural juggernaut.
  • 2017: DAMN. made Kendrick the first rapper to ever win a Pulitzer Prize.
  • 2022: Mr. Morale and The Big Steppers explored vulnerability and personal growth alongside an artistic expansion.
  • 2024: Kendrick returns to his roots after his highly publicized Drake feud with GNX.
